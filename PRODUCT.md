# PRODUCT.md — Liliana Fuentes, Realtor

**register:** brand

## Product purpose
A single-page personal brand site for Liliana Fuentes, a bilingual Realtor® with
Realty ONE Group serving Arizona's West Valley (Goodyear, Buckeye, Avondale) and
statewide. The site's job: build instant trust, communicate warmth + competence,
and convert visitors into a call, a consult booking, or a seminar RSVP.

## Users
- First-time and move-up buyers, many Spanish-speaking or bilingual families.
- Sellers wanting a hands-on, organized agent.
- Seminar attendees ("ImPAKt Your Future" homebuyer workshop, EN + ES).
Mostly arriving from Instagram / TikTok / Facebook bio links on a phone.

## Brand & tone
Warm, grounded, personal, professional. Not corporate-glossy, not flashy. She is
the brand: approachable expert who explains things clearly in either language.
Bilingual identity is core, not decoration. "Hablo español" is a real promise.

## Strategic principles
- Photo-of-Liliana forward: people hire the person, not the brokerage.
- Bilingual woven through, never bolted on.
- Place-rooted: Arizona desert, West Valley specificity over generic luxury.
- Mobile-first: most traffic is social → phone.

## Anti-references (what to avoid)
- Generic "luxury realtor" gold + cream + script-serif template.
- Emoji used as iconography. Sparkle/✦ decoration. Stock-photo gloss.
- Identical icon-card grids. Hero big-number stat template.
- Anything that reads as AI-generated or off-the-shelf.
