The questions buyers
ask me most.

Is the call really free?

Yes. The call is free, and there's zero obligation to buy or list with me. It's 15 minutes to get you clear answers and a real next step.

Do I need a big down payment?

Not always. There are first-time buyer and down-payment assistance programs many people qualify for and never hear about. On our call we'll look at what fits your situation.

My credit isn't perfect. Can I still buy?

Often, yes. And if you're not quite ready, I'll show you the specific steps to get there so you're not stuck guessing. Plenty of my buyers started exactly here.

¿Hablas español?

Sí. Te acompaño en cada paso en el idioma que prefieras, desde la primera llamada hasta la firma final. Every document and decision in Spanish if that's easier for you and your family.

I'm just starting out. Is it too early to call?

The earliest is actually the best time. A 15-minute call now saves months later and helps you avoid the mistakes that cost first-time buyers the most.
